Solar Tracking System using Arduino

The integration of solar tracking systems with Arduino technology is crucial for optimizing the efficiency of solar energy harvesting. Optimizing the effectiveness of solar energy harvesting requires the combination of Arduino technology with sun tracking systems. Although the sun's position varies throughout the day, solar panels work best when they are facing directly toward the sun. Sensors and actuators are used by Arduino-based solar tracking systems to continuously alter the orientation of solar panels so that they are always in line with the sun's rays. By optimizing solar radiation exposure, this dynamic tracking greatly increases the energy production of solar arrays. Due to its adaptability and affordability, Arduino is a great platform for building accurate and responsive solar tracking systems. This increases energy yield and makes solar power a more practical and sustainable option for a wider range of applications.
